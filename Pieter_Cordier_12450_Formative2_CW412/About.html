Background

Campaign

The campaign in Black Ops III is designed to support 4-player cooperative gameplay, allowing for bigger, more open level design and less corridor shooting.
Multiplayer

Multiplayer brings a new momentum based movement system, which utilizes thruster packs to allow users to perform slow boosts into the air. Fast pased FPS game.
Zombies

Black Ops 3 Zombies takes place over a variety of different locations as well as different points in time.Zombies features a new XP progression system.

Rules

Campaign

The campaign features story based missions which a player needs to complete according to the missions objectives.
Multiplayer

Different modes calls for different rules. In COD Multiplayer you get variuos types of modes with different rules.
Zombies

Survival is key! The longer you survive the better. A player earns as he/she kills zombies and plays through the levels.

Features & Content

New Characters

Theres Brand new Specialist Characters like Battery, Prophet and outrider!!!

NukeTown Return

All time fan favorite Call of Duty Black ops 2 map makes it's return.

Blackout

Blackout is a course available for Free Run in Call of Duty: Black Ops III. It is the hardest free run course and is listed as "extreme" difficulty. The world record time is approximately 1:25.

Black Market

The Black Market is where the player can exchange Cryptokeys for Supply Drops.

Downloadable Content

Call of Duty: Black Ops III will feature four DLC (Downloadable Content) packs that will add more Multiplayer and Zombies content to the base game.
